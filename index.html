Why this exists

The Play Store is full of apps. Most of them are fine. A few are brilliant. Many are stuffed with ads and asking for permissions they don't need. I'm not trying to out-ship the giants — I'm trying to make the kind of apps I'd want on my own phone, and the kind I'd be happy to hand to a child.

Every app on my developer page is something I genuinely use, or something I built because I wished it existed. If you find one helpful, that's the whole point.
